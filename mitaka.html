Astronomical data and imagery

Mitaka's built-in credit roll lists all bundled star catalogs (Hipparcos/Tycho, Bright Star, Tully NBG, SDSS, NGC), ephemerides (JPL HORIZONS), and imagery (NASA Blue Marble, 2MASS, ESA/Planck, Axel Mellinger's Milky Way panorama) along with contributing artists and modelers. Access it from the in-app menu.
